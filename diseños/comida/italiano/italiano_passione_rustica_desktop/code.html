Our Story

Founded in 1954 by Nonna Lucia, Passione Rustica began as a small stone cellar where the neighborhood gathered for fresh focaccia and tall tales. Today, we carry that same torch of tradition.

We believe that a recipe is more than instructions; it's a curated heirloom passed down through generations. Our flour comes from the same Umbrian mill we've used for seventy years.
