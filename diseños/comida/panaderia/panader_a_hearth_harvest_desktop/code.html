The Hearth & Harvest

Our journey began with a simple belief: that bread should be honest. We source our grains from local regenerative farms, ensuring every bite supports the soil it grew from.

Named after the two pillars of our craft—the fire that transforms and the earth that provides—we invite you to experience the rhythm of traditional baking in a modern world.
