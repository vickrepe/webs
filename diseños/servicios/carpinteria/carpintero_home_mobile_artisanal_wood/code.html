The Artisanal Legacy

Founded on the principles of traditional joinery and sustainable sourcing, Carpintero represents a rebellion against the disposable era of flat-pack furniture.

We source only FSC-certified hardwoods, allowing each piece to tell the story of the forest it came from. No screws, no staples—only precise dovetails and mortise joints.
